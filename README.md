## 📌 Overview

This project focuses on designing a data-driven dashboard to monitor key performance indicators in the healthcare sector. By leveraging tools like Power BI, SQL, and Power Query, the dashboard offers real-time insights into doctor workload, patient wait times, and departmental efficiency.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Hospitals often lack centralized systems to measure **doctor performance**, **patient wait time**, and **department-level KPIs**, which leads to inefficiencies and delayed care. The goal was to create a scalable reporting tool that would drive better operational decisions and resource allocation.

---

## 🔍 Data Processing

- Cleaned and transformed raw hospital records using **Power Query** and **Excel**.
- Integrated multiple datasets including appointment logs, consultation records, and departmental statistics.
- Modeled relational tables in **Power BI** for seamless dashboard integration.

---

## 📊 Data Visualization

- Developed interactive dashboards in **Power BI**, **Tableau** allowing filtering by department, doctor, and date.
- Used **DAX** to calculate KPIs like **average consultation time**, **patients served**, and **doctor load balancing**.
- Visualized key trends using bar charts, pie charts, and KPIs with conditional formatting for alerts.

---

## 📈 Insights & Trends

- Identified overburdened doctors in the General Medicine department and underutilized ones in Pediatrics.
- Flagged high patient waiting times in certain departments, prompting immediate staffing adjustments.
- Enabled weekly performance tracking by department and individual practitioners.

---

## ✅ Real-Time Impact

- Reduced doctor-patient mismatch through workload balancing.
- Enabled hospital admin to make **data-informed staffing decisions**.
- Improved patient experience by minimizing delays in care.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Skills Used

- **Tableau**, **Power BI**, **SQL**, **Power Query**
- **Data Modeling**, **KPI Calculation**
- **Dashboard Design**, **Healthcare Analytics**

---

## 📌 Conclusion

The Healthcare KPI Dashboard empowered hospital management to optimize doctor allocation and track patient care KPIs. It brought visibility to real-time operational performance and promoted a culture of continuous improvement backed by data.
